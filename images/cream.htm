    Product Details

    With the Dual Action Exfoliator, glycolic acid and microcrystals safely exfoliate dead skin cells. Vitamins and antioxidants fortify skin for a revitalized complexion and newly revealed skin.

    Non-Irritating
    Fragrance Free
    100% Money Back Guarantee
    Free Shipping on US Ground Orders
    Manufactured in the USA

    1.7OZ/ 48.5 g

    Results
    • Glycolic acid and microcrystals safely remove dead skin cells revealing brighter, younger-looking skin
    • Soothing ingredients leave skin with a smoother look and feel
    • Preps skin for powerful stem cell serums to create glowing, youthful skin
    Lifeline Skin Care and its affiliates do not guarantee specific results. Results may vary.
    Usage
    Moisten face and apply a thin layer over face. Massage in circular motion for 30 seconds. Rinse with warm water. Use once or twice a week.
    Ingredients

    KEY INGREDIENTS

    MICROCRYSTALS → Environment-friendly exfoliating agent safely sloughs off dull, dry skin cells in order to texturize and resurface the skin.

    GLYCOLIC ACID → Universally recognized as the best alpha hydroxy acid to promote cellular turnover, glycolic acid is a key ingredient for reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

    VITAMIN E → One of the most powerful anti-oxidants. Vitamin E neutralizes free radicals.

    INGREDIENTS

    Water (Aqua), Polylactic Acid, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Glycerin, Glycolic Acid, Cetearyl Alcohol, Cetearyl Olivate, Sorbitan Olivate, Di-Steareth-100 IPDI, Tocopheryl Acetate, Allantoin, Xanthan Gum, Polysorbate 80, Polysorbate 60, Ethylhexylglycerin, Sodium Hydroxide, Phenoxyethanol.

    Usage

    Dispense 1-2 pumps and apply to clean skin, spreading evenly over face. Repeat every AM and PM.

    Results

    -Supports collagen and elastin production, hyaluronic acid production and brightens the skin.

    -Supports healthy cell metabolism: skin lightening, soothing & smoothing, and firming.

    -Minimizes free radical damage.

    -Stimulates production of hyaluronic acid.

    -Contains a powerful essential fatty acid: linoleic acid. Has dirt and oil-removing properties that help form a moisture barrier.
